    Vision-Language Models for Agriculture

    June 1, 2024

    Overview#

    Exploring how state-of-the-art vision-language models (VLMs) can be adapted and optimized for specialized agricultural tasks. This research contributes to advancing precision agriculture through AI.

    Research Focus#

    • Benchmarking VLMs on agricultural datasets
    • Specialized prompt engineering for farm-related tasks
    • Cross-modal understanding of agricultural imagery
    • Real-world deployment in agricultural systems

    Publications#

    • WACV 2025 - “Leveraging Vision Language Models for Specialized Agricultural Tasks” (IEEE/CVF Winter Conference on Applications of Computer Vision)
    • Research on protein structural prediction (Bioemu-1 benchmarking at AIChE 2025)

    Applications#

    • Crop disease and pest identification
    • Yield estimation
    • Field condition assessment
    • Climate-aware agricultural decision support
