    Improving the Safety and Trustworthiness of Medical AI via Multi-Agent Evaluation Loops

    April, 2026

    Abstract

    A multi-agent iterative-refinement system pairs generator models (DeepSeek R1, Med-PaLM) with evaluator agents (LLaMA 3.1, Phi-4) to align medical LLM outputs to the AMA Principles of Medical Ethics and a five-tier Safety Risk Assessment (SRA-5). The system cut ethical violations by 89% and achieved a 92% risk-downgrade rate across 900 clinical queries spanning nine ethical domains.
