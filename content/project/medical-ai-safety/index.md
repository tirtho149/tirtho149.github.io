## Overview

Developed novel approaches to enhance the safety and ethical compliance of medical LLM outputs. This research addresses critical challenges in deploying AI for healthcare:

- Multi-agent evaluation frameworks using specialized LLMs as evaluator agents
- Iterative refinement against established healthcare standards (AMA-9 Principles)
- Safety Risk Assessment (SRA-5) tier implementation
- Byzantine-resilient aggregation with differential privacy guarantees

## Key Achievements

- **ACM-BCB 2025 Presentation** - Poster presentation at The 16th ACM Conference on Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, and Health Informatics
- Developed framework pairing generative models (DeepSeek R1, Med-PaLM) with evaluator agents (LLaMA 3.1, Phi-4)
- Focus on real-world deployment challenges and regulatory compliance
