## Overview

Collaborating with Professor Soumik Sarkar at the Translational AI Center to explore how LLMs can enhance decision-making processes in finance. This research includes:

- Analyzing how LLMs interpret market signals and business fundamentals
- Studying investor behavior through the lens of AI analysis (Shark Tank data)
- Developing frameworks for AI-assisted financial analysis
- Understanding market orientation and brand storytelling effects on investment decisions

## Key Contributions

- Published research on market orientation and startup pitch evaluation (JBIM, REU 2025)
- Developed LLM-based analytical frameworks for entrepreneurship and investment analysis
- Presented findings at TrAC Day 2025 and REU Summer Symposium 2025
