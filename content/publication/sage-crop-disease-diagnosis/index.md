## Highlights

- Co-built the largest plant-disease image–symptom dataset to date: 335 crops, 1,251 disease classes, ~839K images with source-grounded, expert-validated symptom descriptions.
- Engineered stepwise diagnostic reasoning (anatomical context → symptom-guided narrowing → reference-image comparison → explainable trace).
- Symptom grounding improved accuracy by +16.2 points on average and extends to new crops with no retraining.
- Core component of M.S. thesis research (co-first author).
