## Highlights

- Co-developed AgEval, a 12-task plant-stress phenotyping benchmark for VLMs (Claude, GPT, Gemini, LLaVA).
- Best-model F1 improved 46.24% → 73.37% (8-shot); strategic example selection added +15.38% F1.
- Introduced a coefficient-of-variation metric revealing per-class gaps of 26–58%.
