## Highlights

- Built a multi-agent iterative-refinement loop: generators DeepSeek R1 + Med-PaLM, evaluators LLaMA 3.1 + Phi-4.
- Aligned outputs to AMA Principles of Medical Ethics and a five-tier Safety Risk Assessment (SRA-5).
- Cut ethical violations by 89% and achieved a 92% risk-downgrade rate over 900 clinical queries across nine ethical domains.
